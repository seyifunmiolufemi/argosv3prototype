Set your data start date

This is the date from which Argos will pull and analyse your historical data. All your reports, trend lines, and comparisons will start from this point.

Choose how far back you want your data to go. Most clients start from when they began running ads or 12–24 months ago for meaningful trend analysis.

💡 Not sure what date to pick? A good starting point is 12 months ago. You can always adjust this later in your account settings.
